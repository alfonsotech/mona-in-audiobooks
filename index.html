About Me

My name is Mona Alfonso, and I am a professional voiceover artist with over five years of experience bringing stories to life through my voice. My passion for non-fiction and creative non-fiction projects has led me to work with a diverse range of clients from all over the world.

One of my most memorable experiences was narrating an audiobook for a well-known author whose work had never been translated into English before. As a bilingual voiceover artist, I was able to bring the author's words to life for an entirely new audience, capturing the nuances of the text and making it accessible to English-speaking listeners.

Another project I'm particularly proud of is a corporate video for a technology company that was introducing a new product to the market. The script was technical and complex, but my expertise in non-fiction narration allowed me to communicate the information in a way that was clear and engaging, helping the company to successfully launch their new product.

I've also worked on creative non-fiction projects such as documentary films, podcasts, and online courses. One of the most exciting projects I was involved in was a travel documentary that took me to remote parts of the world, immersing me in different cultures and allowing me to use my voice to tell stories that inspired and educated viewers.

Whether it's a non-fiction or creative non-fiction project, my goal as a voiceover artist is to bring the words on the page to life, using my voice to create a powerful and engaging listening experience for my clients and their audiences.
